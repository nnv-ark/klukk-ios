# KLUKK — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 5 June 2026_

KLUKK is built and published by NNV ehf. ("we", "us"). This policy explains what
KLUKK does — and does not — do with your information. In short: **KLUKK does not
collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.**

## What we collect

Nothing. KLUKK has no account system, no sign-up, and no servers of our own. We do
not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs of any kind.

## Data stored on your device

KLUKK stores your timed sessions and your preferences locally on your device so the
app works between launches. This information never leaves your device except in the
ways you explicitly choose, described below.

## The calendar you choose

When you ask KLUKK to save a session to your iOS Calendar, the app uses Apple's
Calendar (EventKit) framework to create a calendar event on your device. KLUKK only
requests calendar access when you choose the "iOS Calendar" destination, and it only
writes the events you create. These events live in your own calendar, governed by
your iCloud or device settings — not by us. We never see them.

## Exports you initiate

If you choose to export a session as an `.ics` file or append it to a local `.xml`
file, that file is created on your device. Sharing it afterward is entirely under
your control through the standard iOS share sheet.

## Tracking

KLUKK does not track you across apps or websites and contains no tracking
technologies.

## Children

KLUKK is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone,
including children.
